This is my final project for my Digital Media Design class at USFCA. This project was inspired by Rosalia's song "CUUUUuuuuuute" and tells a story through text. Her entire album, "Motomami," has been an inspiration to me since its debut. As someone who unfortunately experienced a frightening motorcycle accident, Rosalia's songs in the Motomami album help me to move forward, living my life and appreciating what I have
